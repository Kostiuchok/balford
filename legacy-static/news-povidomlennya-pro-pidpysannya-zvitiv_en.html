"Balford Ukraine" LLC is pleased to announce

May 12, 2023

"Balford Ukraine" LLC is pleased to announce that reports on research works have been prepared

"Balford Ukraine" LLC is pleased to announce that reports on research works have been prepared and signed in accordance with the recommendations for the implementation of the conclusion of the environmental impact assessment.

Report on the research work titled "On the implementation of the conclusion of the Department of Ecology and Natural Resources of the Lviv Regional State Administration dated May 16, 2019 (case number 03.02.20181252331/2)" based on the results of the environmental impact assessment of the planned activity "Construction of a small hydroelectric station with a capacity of up to 2.0 MW on the Stryi River, Dovge village, Drohobytsky District, Lviv Region". "Balford Ukraine" carried out additional monitoring of the impact of planned activities on the population of ichthyofauna and other components of the biocenosis of the Stryi River and the adjacent territory of the MHP before the start of work in accordance with the permit for the special use of aquatic biological resources in fishery water bodies (their parts). The report includes research fishing on the Stryi River and publication of monitoring results.

Post-project monitoring report on the implementation of the conclusion of the Department of Ecology and Natural Resources of the Lviv Regional State Administration dated May 16, 2019 (case number 20181252331/2). The report is based on the results of the environmental impact assessment of the planned activity "Construction of a small hydroelectric power station with a capacity of up to 2.0 MW on the Stryi River near the village of Dovga, Drohobytsky District, Lviv Region". "Balford Ukraine" carried out monitoring until the start of work in 2021.
